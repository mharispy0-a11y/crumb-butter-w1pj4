Our Story

Handcrafted with Passion, Baked with Soul

Crumb & Butter is an artisanal bakery dedicated to crafting breads and pastries that are both delicious and visually stunning. Our sourdough starter is made from ancient grains, giving our loaves a unique flavor and texture. We're passionate about using only the finest ingredients and traditional baking methods to create a truly authentic experience.

Nestled in the heart of Lahore, DHA Phase 5, we invite you to step into our warm and inviting space, where the aroma of freshly baked goods fills the air, complemented by the soft amber lighting and exposed brick walls.
